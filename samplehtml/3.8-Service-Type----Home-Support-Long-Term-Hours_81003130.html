For clients who are receiving Home Support Long Term service

Clients approved for Long Term Hours are usually registered in the HOME SUPP department using the Waitlist Status until the Schedulers are able to book the first visit. 

When the client status is changed from WAITLIST → ACTIVE LONG TERM - no additional Admission Assessment is required


When the client status is changed from ACTIVE LONG TERM → HOLD - no additional Admission Assessment is required


When the Client is Discharged, Waitlist-Discharged or Deceased  - an Admission Assessment is required

Add a Service

HCC LTC ASSESSMENT PAGE 2


  

Department

  • Add the client to the Home Support Department
  • Set the Department Status to WAITLIST
  • Set the Effective Date to the 3.18 Accepted for Service Date
  • Once visits have been booked by Scheduling- the Dept Status must be changed to ACTIVE - LONG TERM

Using Waitlist to Ensure All Visits Booked

It is recommended to use the Department Status WAITLIST until the scheduling office can process the paperwork and add the client into the CHW schedule.


A large number of reports count the # of Active clients - and clients who are not active but waiting for service to start - should not be counted.

  


Admission Assessment

  • Question 3.8 Service Type

Choose Home Support - LTC

  • Question 3.18 Date Accepted for Service



Enter the Acceptance Date into Question 3.18

  • Question 3.19 Service Start Date Home Support Short Term - LTC


Enter the Service Start Date into Question 3.29.

This may be the same date as Date Accepted but it could be a date after a client has left hospital, decided they wanted services to start, or moved to their own place etc.

Using the Date Accepted for Service Start Date is a temporary workaround until a newer process for tracking & collecting the Actual Start Date can be recorded because it is important for Scheduling & Billing that the Dept Status is not set to Active until the Schedulers are able to book the first visit. Therefore, use the Dept Status Waitlist until the visits are booked

  • Question 3.22 Approved Hours per Month Home Support - LTC

    • Monthly Home Support Hours will be calculated as weekly hours x 52 ÷ 12 (rounding up when needed)

    • Assessors will round up to the next full hour to avoid using decimals (Example: 4.33hrs would round up to 5hrs)

Enter the number of Approved Hours per Month for Home Support.


This number will be the MAXIMUM number of Hours to be Scheduled. This does not mean that the client will receive this MAX hours every month


This calculation is a workaround guideline so that Visit Hours Approved vs Visit Hours Delivered can be measured and errors audited. It is better to be slightly over on these hours than under.
